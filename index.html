Do not ignore the water!

Your new car should not leak.

Jeep Wranglers are known for leaking water into the interior. Many owners take this as fact and simply move on with their lives; they deal with the annoyance of water on the floor or they try to fix it themselves. If you have a Wrangler that leaks and is under the manufacturer's warranty, you do not have to just accept this design flaw. There are resources available to you that allow you to force Chrysler to take care of the issue.

There are resources available to you!
