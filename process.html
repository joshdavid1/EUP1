The Lemon Law Process

The process of finding out if your vehicle is a lemon can be confusing and convoluted.

There is a set of rules you must follow, hoops you must jump through, and paperwork you must file. I have written out the basic steps below so that you can get a high-level view of the end-to-end flow.

How do I know if my Jeep Wrangler is a lemon?

Here are the criteria that must be met:

  1. The vehicle has a factory hard or soft top installed.
  2. You notice water inside the vehicle after it has been sitting out in the rain, and it happens every time.
  3. You have taken it to a Chrysler-authorized service dealership at least four times to have the problem fixed.
  4. The dealership admits in writing that they cannot fix the problem.
